# Expense Approval — Larkspur Financial

Owner: Finance (Controller: Priya Nair)
Last reviewed: 2026-03-22

## Thresholds
- Under $500: auto-approved, receipt required.
- $500–$5,000: manager approval.
- Over $5,000: Controller approval, with a business justification.
- Over $25,000: requires a purchase order and CFO approval.

## Procedure
1. Submit the expense with a receipt and category.
2. Route to the approver implied by the amount.
3. Approver checks the budget line has room before approving.
4. Finance posts approved expenses weekly; rejected ones return to the submitter with a reason.

## Rules
- No expense is reimbursed without a receipt.
- Splitting one purchase into smaller expenses to dodge a threshold is prohibited.
