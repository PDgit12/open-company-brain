# Customer Support FAQ — Larkspur Financial

Owner: Customer Operations
Last reviewed: 2026-05-28

**How long do refunds take?** Once approved, refunds reach the original payment method in 3–5 business days.

**Can I change the bank account on file?** Yes, through identity verification. Beneficiary changes for wires go through the verified-vendor process.

**What are your support hours?** 24/7 for money-movement issues; 9am–9pm ET for everything else.

**Why was my transaction held?** Transactions over $50,000, or that trip a fraud rule, are held for review and usually clear within 1 business day.

**Who do I escalate a stuck wire to?** Support escalates to Treasury Operations; wires over $50,000 are reconciled same day.

**Is my data shared?** Customer PII is never sold or copied off production. See the Security & Access Policy.
