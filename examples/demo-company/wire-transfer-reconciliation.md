# Wire Transfer & Reconciliation — Larkspur Financial

Owner: Treasury Operations (Director: Sam Okafor)
Last reviewed: 2026-04-18

## Policy
- Every outbound wire **over $50,000** must be **reconciled against the bank statement the same business day**.
- Wires over $250,000 require dual authorization (maker + checker) before release.
- All wires are logged with the initiator, approver, beneficiary, and reference.

## Procedure (how a wire is reconciled)
1. Pull the day's outbound wires from the payments system.
2. Match each wire to the bank's confirmation by reference number and amount.
3. For any wire over $50,000, confirm same-day settlement and tick it reconciled.
4. Escalate any unmatched or late wire to the Treasury Director within 1 hour.
5. File the reconciliation report; unreconciled wires roll into the next-day exceptions queue.

## Rules
- A wire over $50,000 **not reconciled same day** is a control exception and must be flagged.
- Beneficiary bank details may only be changed through the verified-vendor process.
