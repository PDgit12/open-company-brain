# Refund Policy — Larkspur Financial

Owner: Customer Operations (VP: Dana Reyes)
Last reviewed: 2026-05-01

## Thresholds
- Refunds **up to $2,000** may be issued by any support agent.
- Refunds **$2,000–$10,000** require a team lead's approval in the ticket.
- Refunds **over $10,000** require **VP of Customer Operations sign-off** before issue. No exceptions.

## Procedure (how a refund is handled)
1. Verify the original transaction in the ledger and confirm the customer's identity.
2. Check the refund amount against the thresholds above.
3. If approval is required, request it in the ticket and wait for written sign-off.
4. Issue the credit to the original payment method only — never a different account.
5. Record the refund reason code and link the approving person.

## Rules
- Refunds are issued to the **original payment method** only.
- A refund over $10,000 without VP sign-off is a **policy violation** and must be flagged.
- Duplicate refunds on the same transaction are blocked by the ledger.
