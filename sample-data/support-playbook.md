# Support Playbook

Tiers: T1 triage (first response under 2 business hours), T2 technical, T3 engineering escalation.

Refund requests: verify the order, check the refund policy, issue goodwill credit up to $2,000 yourself; anything larger routes to Finance per the refund policy.

Angry customer: acknowledge, do not argue, summarize their issue back to them, give a concrete next step and a time.

Bug reports: reproduce, capture logs, file in the tracker with severity, link the ticket.

Escalate a SEV1 (customer robots down) to on-call immediately - do not wait.
