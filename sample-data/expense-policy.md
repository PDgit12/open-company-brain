# Expense Policy

Meals on business travel: up to $75/day with receipts.
Software: purchases over $200 need IT approval (annual per-seat too).
Hardware: over $1,000 needs IT director approval.
Client entertainment: up to $500, manager approval, itemized receipt required.
Conferences: up to $2,500 including travel and ticket, needs manager + finance approval.
Home office: one-time $500 stipend for new hires, no approval.
Submit within 30 days through the Finance portal.
