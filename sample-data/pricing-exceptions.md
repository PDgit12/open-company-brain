# Pricing Exception Process

List price holds unless an exception is approved.
- Up to 10%: any Account Executive, no approval.
- 10-20%: Sales Manager approval.
- 20-30%: VP Sales approval plus a margin review by Finance.
- Over 30%: CEO approval, rare, only for strategic logos.

Multi-year prepay earns an automatic 8% in addition to the above.

Every exception must be recorded in the CRM opportunity with the approver's name and the business justification BEFORE the quote is sent.
